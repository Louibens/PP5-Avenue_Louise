About Us

Passionate about eco-conscious living, I'm on a journey to reimagine the possibilities of everyday objects with an awareness of the greater impact on the world around us. I am constantly on the look out to source unwanted fabrics to breathe new life into them, creating unique, sustainable textile art pieces.
Weaving has become my meditation, a mindful practice that connects me to the beauty of each thread.
But my passion doesn't end there. I dream of teaching future generations the art of crafting, not just for aesthetics but with a deep consideration for our planet's impact.
I want to empower them to create beautiful pieces that stand the test of time. and inspire them to repair, not replace, so that together, we can weave a more beautiful and sustainable future.
Have a look at the workshops section to view our upcoming workshops in dublin.
We strive to operate with no waste which constantly challenges our creativity and pushes our imagination to think outside the box.
